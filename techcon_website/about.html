Our History: From a Small Idea to a Global Stage

TechCon was first held in 2010, starting as a small, informal gathering of five local developers in a co-working space in San Francisco. Our founders believed in the power of shared knowledge and open-source collaboration. Each year, word spread, and by 2015, we had outgrown our local roots to become a regional must-attend event.

Today, TechCon hosts thousands of attendees from over 50 countries, but our core value—connecting brilliant minds—remains the same.

Our Mission: Driving Innovation and Accessibility

Our driving principles are centered on three core goals:

  • **Democratize Knowledge:** Provide access to cutting-edge research and practices regardless of geographical location.
  • **Foster Inclusion:** Create a welcoming environment for developers and innovators of all backgrounds and experience levels.
  • **Inspire the Future:** Showcase groundbreaking technology and foster networking opportunities that lead to the next generation of tech breakthroughs.

Notable Past Speakers

We are proud to have hosted some of the most influential figures in the technology space. Here are a few highlights:

Dr. Anya Sharma (AI Ethics)

Dr. Sharma delivered the 2022 keynote on "The Algorithmic Conscience," challenging developers to build more responsible machine learning systems. Her work has significantly influenced industry standards.

Mr. Ben Carter (Cloud Architecture)

In 2023, Mr. Carter presented a deep dive into next-generation serverless architectures, demonstrating how to scale applications to billions of users with near-zero latency.
